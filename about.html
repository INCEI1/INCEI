Our Goal

The Indo-Nepal Cultural Exchange Initiative (INCEI) Japan aims to promote cultural exchange and understanding between the people of India, Nepal, and Japan. Our goal is to create opportunities for cultural dialogue, collaboration, and friendship.

Establishment

INCEI Japan was established in 2018 by a group of enthusiastic individuals who shared a passion for fostering cross-cultural connections. Since then, we have organized numerous events, workshops, and initiatives to promote cultural exchange and appreciation in Japan.
