Socio-Cultural Program

Date: May 28th, 2024

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Occasion: Nepali Republic Day

Description:Step into a world of cultural celebration and unity at the Indo-Nepal Cultural Initiative's (INCEI) special event in Tokyo on May 28th, 2024! Commemorating Nepali Republic Day, this vibrant program is designed to build strong bonds of brotherhood and intimacy among the Nepalese and Indian communities in Japan. Featuring mesmerizing cultural performances, engaging interactive sessions, and insightful discussions, the event promises a delightful experience that celebrates our shared heritage and fosters mutual understanding. We are thrilled to welcome Hon'ble Maya Shrestha, Provincial Member of Bagmati Province, Government of Nepal, and renowned youth leader of the Nepali Congress Party, as our esteemed Special Guest. Join us for an unforgettable celebration of cultural diversity, friendship, and harmony!

Organizer: INCEI Japan
